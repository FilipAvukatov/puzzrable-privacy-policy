Privacy Policy

At Puzzrable, I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while using the app.

Information I Collect

Puzzrable itself does not collect or store personally identifiable information such as your name, address, or contact details. However, if you choose to contact me through the in-app feedback form, your email address and optional name will be sent securely via Formspree, a trusted third-party service that processes contact submissions.

The app may also store small pieces of data locally on your device (for example, to save your progress in puzzles or parables). This data never leaves your device.

How I Use Your Information

Any data stored is used solely to improve the user experience and ensure the app functions properly. I never share or sell any user data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Puzzrable is built using technologies like Expo and React Native. These may collect limited technical data (such as crash logs or performance metrics) following their own privacy policies.

Data Security

Your local data is stored securely on your own device and is not transmitted elsewhere.

Children’s Privacy

Puzzrable is suitable for all audiences. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect app improvements. The “Last Updated” date will always show the most recent version.

Data Deletion Request

If you have submitted any feedback or personal information through the Puzzrable app and would like your data to be deleted, please contact me at avukatov.filip@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

I will delete your data (such as any feedback messages or contact information) within 30 days of receiving your request. I do not store any user accounts or persistent personal data within the app.
